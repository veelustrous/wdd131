About Belgium

Belgium mixes medieval charm with modern culture. From the ornate Grand Place in Brussels to the canals of Bruges, you’ll find chocolate shops, world-class museums, and cozy cafes. The official languages include Dutch, French, and German, and the food scene is a delicious mix — waffles, fries, and mussels among favorites.
