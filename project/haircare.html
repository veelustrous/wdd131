Haircare FAQ

Use lukewarm water and mild shampoo. Gently detangle from tips to roots. Air dry on a stand.

Style only when necessary to reduce wear. Avoid excessive heat. Always use heat protectant if styling with tools.

Place on a wig stand in a cool, dry place. Keep away from sunlight to maintain color and texture.
